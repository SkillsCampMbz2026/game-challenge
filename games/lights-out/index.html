Lights Out

Turn off every light on the 5×5 grid in as few moves as possible.

How to play
  • The board is a 5×5 grid of lights, each either on or off.
  • Clicking a light toggles that light and its direct neighbors above, below, left and right (if they exist).
  • The goal is to get every light off using as few clicks as possible.
  • Every puzzle is randomly generated but guaranteed solvable.
  • Use keyboard Tab and Enter/Space to play without a mouse.
