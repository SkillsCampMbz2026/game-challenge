Marble Machine

Place ramps, platforms and springs so the ball rolls into the basket.

How to play
  • The ball starts at the flag in the top-left and falls with gravity.
  • Your job: build a path of ramps, platforms and springs that steers it into the green basket.
  • Drag a part from the tray onto an empty cell — or click a part in the tray to arm it, then click an empty cell to place it.
  • Click any placed part to remove it and return it to the tray. Each part type has a limited supply.
  • Ramps redirect a falling ball sideways. Platforms are flat rests. Springs give the ball an extra bounce.
  • Press Run to start the simulation. If the ball misses, tweak your contraption and run it again. Reset clears everything.
  • Keyboard: Tab to a part then Enter to arm it; Tab to the board, use arrow keys to move the cursor and Enter to place or remove.
