Tower of Hanoi

Move every disk from the left tower to the right tower, one at a time.

How to play
  • The goal is to move the entire stack of disks from Tower A to Tower C.
  • You may only move one disk at a time, and only the top disk of a tower.
  • A disk can never be placed on top of a smaller disk.
  • To move a disk: click a tower to pick up its top disk, then click another tower to drop it there — or simply drag the top disk to another tower.
  • Try to solve it in the minimum number of moves shown below!
